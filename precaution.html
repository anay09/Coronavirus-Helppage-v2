Precautions

This page will reveal the precautions you should take to stay as safe as you can from this disease.


What should you do to not get COVID-19?


DOs
  • Stay at least 2m away from everyone you do not live with, when outside your home.
  • Wash your hands thoroughly with soap and water for at least 20 seconds
  • Use had sanitiser gel if soap and water is not available.
  • Wash your hands as soon as you get home.
  • Cover your mouth and nose with a tissue or your sleeve when you cough or sneeze.
  • Put used tissue in the bin immediately and then wash your hands afterwards for at least 20 seconds.
DON'Ts

  • Do not under any circumstances touch your eyes, nose or mouth if your hands are not clean.
  • Try not to touch anything when you go outside
