Symptoms of COVID-19

This section of the website will tell you the symptoms of COVID-19.

What are the symptoms of COVID-19?

The main symptoms of coronavirus are:

  • A high temperature
  • A new, continuous cough
  • A loss or change to your sense of smell or taste

Most people with coronavirus have at least one of these symptoms. However, there could be other symptoms; these are the main symptoms.



If you experience these symptoms, you should:

  • Stay at home (self-isolate) - under any circumstances do not leave the house.
  • Get a test - Get a test to see if you have coronavirus
However, these are very common symptoms for the common cold or hayfever, so if these symptoms are not very severe, then you may not have to worry much.
