What is COVID-19?

COVID-19 is a new infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. The majority of people with the COVID-19 virus experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment. Older people and those with underlying medical problems are highly likely to be infected by this disease. Examples of these medical problems could be :

  • Cardiovascular disease
  • Diabetes
  • Chronic respiratory disease
  • Cancer
